How to use this tool

Choose a database from the sidebar, write SQL in the editor, and click Run to see results. Click Review to get AI feedback. Drag the divider between editor and results to reshape your workspace. Export your query or results anytime using the toolbar buttons.

🗄️

The PixelMetrics database

PixelMetrics is the fictional SaaS company at the center of Walter's LinkedIn Learning course. It includes four tables — customers, orders, products, and regions — with ~1,200 rows of realistic data built to cover every chapter. Practice directly against the queries from the course.

🔬

Digital Evidence database

A forensic SQL database for analysts who want to push beyond the basics. Complex joins, subqueries, and aggregations drawn from investigative scenarios. Use it when you're ready to stress-test your SQL reasoning against real-world complexity.

🤖

Walter AI feedback

The Review button sends your query and the full database schema to Walter AI, which returns personalized feedback: is the query correct, is the style clean, and what's the one thing to improve? Built to accelerate learning without replacing the thinking.
